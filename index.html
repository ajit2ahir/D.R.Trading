Our Products

Explore our range of onions, garlic, and dehydrated goods sourced directly from trusted farms.

Red onion

Featuring a vibrant purple skin and crisp white flesh, red onions offer a mild, peppery flavor that is perfect for raw applications. Their crunchy texture and striking color make them an essential garnish for salads, sandwiches, and tacos.

White onion

Characterized by their papery white skin and snowy flesh, these onions offer a high water content and a bold, classic bite. They are exceptionally versatile, holding their shape well in cooked dishes while providing a sharp crunch when served raw.

White Dehydrated onion

Our white dehydrated onions provide consiatent flavor and crunch without the labor of pealing or chopping. perfect for high-volume cooking, they offer a concentrated savory profile that integrates seamlessly into sauces, batters, and dry rubs.

Garlic

Renowned for its pungent aroma and deep savory complexity, garlic is the foundational heart of global cuisine. Whether roasted for a buttery sweetness or minced raw for a spicy kick it is an indispensable ingredient for elevating any savory dish.

Dehydrated Garlic

OUR dehydrated garlic offers a concentrated flavor profile that is more mellow and nutty than raw cloves, ensuring a consistent taste in every dish. it is the perfect shelf-stable solution for creating dry rubs, seasonings, and batters where moisture control is key.

Red Dehydrated onion

A vibrant, flavor-packed alternative to fresh onions that offers a unique balance of sweetness and sharp aroma. Perfect for garnishing and cooking alike, these dehydrated flakes provide instant color and a gourmet touch to any recipe.

White Onion Powder

White Onion Powder is a versatile, free-flowing spice made from finely ground, dehydrated white onions, offering a concentrated, pungent onion flavor without the moisture or texture of fresh onions.

Pink Onion Powder

Pink Onion Powder is a versatile culinary ingredient made from finely ground, dehydrated pink onions. It provides a sweet, mild onion flavor and aroma without the moisture or texture of fresh onions.

Garlic Powder

Garlic powder is an off-white to yellowish-brown powder. Its flavor is pungent but mellows and sweetnes significantly when cooked. unlike fresh garlic, the drying process removes the moisture and some of the sharper, more volatile compounds, resulting in a slightly diffrent flavor profile that is less assertive when raw.

Red Onion Powder

Red Onion Powder is a versatile, free-flpwing seasoning made from ground, dehydrated red onions. It offers a pungent, sweet, and slightly milder onion flavor compared to White onion powder, with hints of sharpness characteristic of red onions.
